Frequently Asked Questions

Is my email data safe?

Yes! InboxPlus / InboxPro processes all data locally on your device. We never store, transmit, or have access to your emails or personal information.

How does one-click unsubscribe work?

Many newsletters include a special header (List-Unsubscribe) that allows instant unsubscription. The App uses this feature to unsubscribe you with a single tap.

Can I recover deleted emails?

Deleted emails are moved to your Gmail Trash folder, where they remain for 30 days before permanent deletion. You can recover them from Trash during this period.

Why do I need to sign in with Google?

Google Sign-In is required to securely access your Gmail account. We use Google's official OAuth 2.0 authentication, which means we never see your password.

How do I revoke app access?

You can revoke InboxPlus's access anytime by visiting your Google Account settings > Security > Third-party apps with account access, and removing InboxPlus.

The app is scanning slowly. What can I do?

Scanning speed depends on your inbox size and internet connection. For large inboxes (50,000+ emails), the initial scan may take a few minutes. Subsequent scans will be faster due to caching.

Does InboxPlus work with other email providers?

Currently, InboxPlus only supports Gmail accounts. Support for other email providers may be added in future updates.
