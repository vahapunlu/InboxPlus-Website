Privacy Policy

InboxPlus / InboxPro - Email Cleaner

Last updated: January 4, 2026

Introduction

InboxPlus / InboxPro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application InboxPlus / InboxPro - Email Cleaner ("App") handles your information.

Key Point: InboxPlus / InboxPro processes all email data locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your emails or personal data to any external servers.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we do NOT do:

  • We do NOT read or store the content of your emails
  • We do NOT collect your email addresses or contact lists
  • We do NOT track your email activity
  • We do NOT share any data with third parties
  • We do NOT use your data for advertising purposes
  • We do NOT have access to your Google account password

How the App Works

The App uses Google Sign-In to authenticate your account. When you sign in:

  • Google provides a secure access token to the App
  • The App uses this token to access your Gmail data through Google's official API
  • All data processing happens locally on your device
  • Email metadata (sender names, email counts) is displayed only within the App
  • No email content or metadata is ever sent to our servers

Google API Services

InboxPlus uses Google API Services to access your Gmail account. Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data Storage

The App may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Your authentication token (to keep you signed in)
  • App preferences and settings
  • Cached email sender information (for faster loading)

This data is stored only on your device and is deleted when you sign out or uninstall the App.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In: For secure authentication
  • Google Gmail API: To access your email data
  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements (AdMob may collect device identifiers for ad personalization)

Advertising

InboxPlus displays advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain device information to provide personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings. For more information, please see Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Secure OAuth 2.0 authentication through Google
  • No storage of passwords or sensitive credentials
  • All communication with Google servers uses HTTPS encryption
  • Local data is protected by your device's security features

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Sign out of the App at any time
  • Revoke the App's access through your Google Account settings
  • Delete all local data by uninstalling the App
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
